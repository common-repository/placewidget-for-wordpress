Embed Foursquare venue widgets from PlaceWidget.com on your Wordpress blog

== Description ==

People love you on Foursquare, you've got the hottest place to hang out in town.
Why not show it off? Add a Foursquare venue widget to your website and everyone
can see how popular your place is and read the tips people have left.

This plugin simplifies the process of creating and adding a PlaceWidget from PlaceWidget.com
to your Wordpress blog. You can configure your PlaceWidget right in the Wordpress
Dashboard, then embed it in your sidebar with a standard Wordpress widget.

== Installation ==

1. Download and extract the plugin, then copy the resulting folder and all files
within to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory on your server
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Configure the widget though Settings > PlaceWidget in your Wordpress Dashboard
4. Add the widget to your theme through Appearance > Widgets
